VLSI engineer focused on RTL design and verification, with hands-on experience in building robust digital systems from microarchitecture to validation. My work spans SystemVerilog RTL, CDC/reset-safe design, functional verification, SoC and bus integration, protocol development, FPGA acceleration, and implementation-aware hardware design. Through my portfolio and VLSIVault projects, I aim to build hardware that is not only functionally correct, but also structured, scalable, and engineer-friendly. I am especially interested in roles involving RTL design, design verification, and digital system architecture, where strong fundamentals, disciplined execution, and clear technical storytelling are equally valued.
